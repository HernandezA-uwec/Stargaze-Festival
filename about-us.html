Who Are We?

Stargaze Festival is one of the newest music festivals in the Twin Cities Area, hosting over 25+ different acts. The festival tries focusing in rising indie artists, mainly in genres such as RnB, Pop, and Rap. Stargaze also hopes on closing a hole in the music scene in Minnesota, with the different kinds of genres throughout the festival grounds (hosted in the State Fair) it hopes to have a little something for everyone.

This 3 day festival is hoping to increase the economy in Minneapolis-St. Paul. with the sale of tickets, as well as the different Local Street food vendors, and businesses who are able to place themselves inside the festival.
