Food Vendors

Stargaze Festival prides itself on the local food vendors that place themselves in the festival grounds, from burgers, to mexican food to thai food, we’ve made sure to offer something for everyone

  • La Hacienda - Mexican Food (Tacos & Quesadillas)
  • The Galloping Goose - UK Bar Food (Fish & Chips)
  • Thai Cuisine Xpress - Thai Food (Pad Thai & Spring Rolls)
  • La Michoacana - Ice Cream & Paletas
  • Big Mike's BBQ - BBQ Style (Ribs & Brisket)
  • Burger King - Our Main Sponsor (Burgers & Nuggets)

Small Business

We Also pride ourselves in our small local businesses that get the chance to be in our festival grounds, from jewelry, to snacks, T-shirts and Hoodies to even Jacuzzis, you will find the most variety in most of our vendors that we’ll make sure you find something that you love to take home
